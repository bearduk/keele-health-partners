About us

KHP is a partnership platform designed to move ideas into action.

Keele Health Partners (KHP) brings together health, academic, community, and business partners to tackle some of the biggest health challenges facing Staffordshire and the North Midlands. Our goal is simple: to turn great ideas and research into real-world solutions that make a positive difference to people’s lives.

Connect

Bring organisations together around shared health and care challenges.

Develop

Create opportunities for collaborative research, innovation, and funding.

Deliver

Support practical solutions that benefit patients, communities, and systems.

Why partner with KHP

KHP is designed to encourage collaboration from external organisations, including businesses, health and care providers, and wider sector partners. We want to create practical routes into joint work, not just describe our ambitions.

Research areas

Priority areas aligned to real health and care challenges.

We are aligning our work with the NHS Long Term Plan, shifting care closer to home, embracing digital technology, and focusing on prevention. These priority areas create the strongest opportunities for collaboration and impact.

Mental Health

Supporting mental wellbeing through research, digital tools, and community engagement. We want to create practical solutions that help people at every stage of life.

Life Sciences & Precision Medicine

Driving biomedical research, including neuroscience, pharmacology, and bioengineering, and turning discoveries into personalised, preventative care.

Healthy Ageing & Long-Term Conditions

Building on Keele’s expertise in musculoskeletal health and primary care to find better ways to support ageing populations and manage chronic conditions in community settings.

Technology, Digital Health & Data Innovation

Using technology, data, AI, and digital platforms to improve care, empower self-management, and free up clinical time while working with digital and regional innovation partners.

Our impact

What success looks like for Keele Health Partners.

By bringing together partners from a diverse range of backgrounds, KHP aims to make a stronger, broader contribution to the communities it serves. These five pillars define the impact we are working to achieve.

01

Equitable Access

Making sure the benefits of health and care research reach everyone in our communities.

02

Impactful Research & Innovation

Delivering high-quality, co-produced research which puts evidence-based innovation at the heart of care.

03

Accelerated Translation

Moving discoveries into practice quickly so they can be adopted across the system.

04

Workforce & Regional Prosperity

Developing skills, supporting clinical-academic careers, and helping to grow the local economy through health innovation.

05

Sustainable Commercial Partnerships

Building new collaboration through working with sector-leading organisations.

Public Partnerships

Working with people and communities, not just around them.

Public Partnerships will provide a dedicated route for involving people, communities, and lived experience in shaping KHP’s work. For now, this section signals how KHP intends to build collaboration that reflects the needs and experiences of the communities affected by health and care challenges.

Planned focus

  • Community-informed research and innovation
  • Co-production and inclusive engagement approaches
  • Better routes for civic and VCSE collaboration

Training

Potential themes
